---
title: Computational Methods
subtitle: Numerical algorithms for particle and grid-based multiphysics simulation
---

This research field focuses on the development of stable, efficient, and reproducible numerical schemes for particle and grid-based methods. Emphasis is placed on boundary treatment, accuracy improvement, and GPU-accelerated implementations for large-scale simulations.

## Research Topics

### Particle-Based Numerical Schemes
- Advanced wall boundary treatments for SPH  
- Density diffusion-based and reconstruction-enhanced formulations  
- Stability and consistency improvements  

### Heat & Transport Modeling
- Heat transfer models near rigid and deformable boundaries  
- Conjugate heat transfer in particle frameworks  
- Multiphase and multiphysics transport modeling  

### High-Performance & Reproducible Computing
- CUDA-based parallel implementations  
- Contact detection and scalable data structures  
- Method verification, benchmarking, and reproducibility workflows
