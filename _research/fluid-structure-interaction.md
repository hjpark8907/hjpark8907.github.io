---
title: Fluid–Structure Interaction
subtitle: Physics-based multiphysics coupling for free-surface and deformable systems
---

This research area develops robust and high-fidelity coupling strategies for free-surface flows and deformable structures. We focus on particle–structure interaction under impact, sloshing, and transient loading conditions, integrating meshfree and grid-based formulations for engineering-scale simulations.

## Research Topics

### SPH–FEM Coupling Frameworks
- Partitioned and monolithic SPH–FEM coupling strategies  
- Impact, sloshing, and fluid-induced structural response  
- Stability enhancement for strongly coupled systems  

### Interface & Boundary Modeling
- Segment-based interface treatment models  
- Free-surface detection and pressure stabilization  
- Imposition of no-slip/free slip boundary conditions 

### Verification & Engineering Validation
- Canonical FSI benchmark validation  
- Experimental comparison for impact and sloshing cases  
- Energy consistency and conservation analysis
